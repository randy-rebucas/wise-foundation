## What you see

**Maintenance mode** shows an informational screen instead of the dashboard. This protects data during upgrades or incidents.

## What to do

- Wait for an **ADMIN** or operator to clear maintenance.
- Avoid refreshing aggressively; status updates when maintenance ends.
- If you believe maintenance was enabled by mistake, contact your **ADMIN** with time and URL.

## Related

Administrators should document their own runbook for toggling maintenance in this environment (env flags, deployment pipeline, etc.).
