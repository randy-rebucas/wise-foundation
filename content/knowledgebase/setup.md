## Purpose

The **setup** route is used during **initial installation** or controlled re-provisioning: creating baseline admin users, branches, or seed configuration depending on how this deployment is configured.

## Recommended sequence

1. Complete **setup** steps in the UI or CLI your deployment documents.
2. Sign in as **ADMIN**.
3. Create **branches** and **organizations** to match your business structure.
4. Create **users** with correct roles and branch assignments.
5. Load **products** and **inventory**, then validate **POS** with a test sale.

## Handoff to operations

After setup, day-to-day work moves to the [dashboard](/help/dashboard), [branches](/help/admin-branches), and [organizations](/help/admin-organizations) guides.
