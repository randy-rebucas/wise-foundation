## Account

Update **name**, **avatar**, or **password** when the forms are available. Use a strong unique password per policy.

## Tenant display

**App name** and branding may come from **tenant settings** loaded for all users—changes are usually admin-only.

## Session

Use **Sign out** in the sidebar on shared computers. Session duration follows your NextAuth / deployment configuration.
